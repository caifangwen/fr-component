Key Properties of PP Non-Woven Fabric
  • Tear-resistant and puncture-tolerant
  • Moisture-resistant — does not absorb water
  • Breathable — air passes through micro-fiber structure
  • Chemically inert — no dye leach under normal conditions
  • Recyclable within PP streams (#5 plastic)
  • Available from 60 gsm to 120 gsm heavy-duty

Pro tip: 80-100 gsm covers 90% of retail use cases. Only go heavier if you need hardware or bulk grocery load capacity above 12 kg.

International sample shipping via DHL Express is complimentary for all first-time orders. Physical samples ship within 5-7 business days of digital proof approval.

PP non-woven fabric is not biodegradable. Its environmental benefit only holds when the bags are genuinely reused and properly disposed of. Minimum 11 reuses required to offset one plastic bag.

§ 8 · Vertical Flowchart
class="hh-flow"
1
Day 1–2 Polymer Extrusion

PP pellets are melted and extruded through spinnerets. Filament diameter determines the final fabric's feel and tensile strength.

2
Day 2–3 Web Laying & Thermal Bonding

Filaments are laid randomly onto a moving belt, then passed through heated calendar rollers — no adhesive required.

3
Day 3–5 Printing & Color Application

Branding applied via screen printing, offset, heat transfer, or BOPP lamination for photo-quality full-bleed coverage.

4
Day 5–12 Cutting, Sewing & QC

Die-cut or laser-cut panels sewn with reinforced seams. AQL 2.5 random sampling before shipment.

§ 16 · FAQ Accordion
class="hh-faq" + details.hh-faq-item
Can you accept orders below your standard MOQ?

Yes, in most cases. Small-batch production is available for select product lines, sometimes as low as 100 pcs. Minimum order surcharges may apply. Send us your quantity and we'll advise on the most cost-effective route.

How do you handle color accuracy across large runs?

We use Pantone Solid Coated as the production standard — not screen color. Our calibrated inkroom is tested daily. Bulk order color tolerance is ±5 Delta E vs. the signed physical sample.

What happens if my delivery is delayed?

We issue a written delay notice a minimum of 10 days before the original delivery date, with two alternative solutions (partial shipment, expedited shipping upgrade). Our contracts include a delivery guarantee clause.

What certifications do your factories hold?

BSCI, ISO 14001, ISO 9001, OEKO-TEX (select materials). Certificates are available on request for all 9 production bases.
